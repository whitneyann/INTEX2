Accessibility Statement

The homepage of this website has been designed according to accessibility standards. In order to aid those who are dyslexic, all fonts are not pure black, but rather a very dark gray. All fonts are a minimum of 16px. Contrast ratios of the colors used on the homepage have been changed in order o meet the accessibility standards. All graphics have been edited to include an 'alt' description. Hierarchical text settings have been implemented as well. I also added a "skip to main content" link
